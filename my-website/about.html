About

Waddup World!

I'm a poet and musician born and raised in the county of dade—Miami, FL.

I make narrative rap about whatever I want to understand—whether it's the perspective of future space traverlers or someone that makes tea for a living.
My work can be thought of as research in the form of rap. Or just storytelling.

I'm also a creative programmer (that's just fancy for poet).
I develop hardware and software tools for music education and overall creative expression.

Challenging and exploring what's around me,
inviting others to join in the process, and creating interactive experiences is at the heart of what I do.
